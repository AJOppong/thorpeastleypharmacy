About Thorpe Astley Pharmacy

Your Trusted Friendly Community Pharmacy in the United Kingdom

Thorpe Astley Pharmacy is an NHS registered pharmacy that has been in operation since 2007. We offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the health needs of our community. Located in Thorpe Astley, Blaby, Leicestershire, we work hard to provide our patients with the services they need.

We firmly believe that serving the community requires constant commitment and a professional attitude towards healthcare. Our aim is to provide our patients and customers with comprehensive information and guidance about their health and well-being.

Remaining patient-focused, we offer easy access to a wide range of pharmaceutical and medical supplies. All staff continuously work hard to maintain and promote a high-quality healthcare service accessible to all individuals.
